A Stolen Horizon

2023 Prabhakar Pachpute’s

Prabhakar Pachpute’s ’A Stolen Horizon’ is part of Delinking and Relinking, New collection display at the Van Abbemuseum, Eindhoven, Netherlands. The exhibition is Curated by Charles Esche, Diana Franssen, Steven ten Thije and is on view until July 01, 2024.
